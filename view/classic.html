About the Classic 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is a perfect blend of vintage aesthetics and modern engineering. This motorcycle pays homage to the golden era of motorcycling while incorporating contemporary technology for enhanced safety and performance. With its distinctive retro styling, chrome accents, and the legendary Royal Enfield thump, the Classic 350 captures the essence of classic motorcycling.

Built on the proven J-platform, the Classic 350 features improved refinement, better fuel efficiency, and enhanced reliability. Whether you're cruising through city streets or embarking on weekend adventures, this motorcycle delivers a smooth, comfortable ride with an unmistakable character that turns heads wherever you go. The Classic 350 is not just a motorcycle; it's a statement of style and a celebration of motorcycling heritage.
