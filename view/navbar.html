Built Like a Gun

Royal Enfield is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production. Our motorcycles are engaging, intuitive and above all fun to ride. We have stuck to our basic principle of making simple, honest motorcycles that are easy to use and engaging to ride.

Our journey has taken us from the gentle lanes of England to the dusty trails of India, and now to the world. We continue to build modern classics that are evocative, engaging and fun to ride.
