About the Standard 350

The Royal Enfield Standard 350 represents the purest form of motorcycling heritage. Born from the legacy of the iconic Bullet series, this motorcycle embodies the timeless appeal that has made Royal Enfield a legend in the motorcycle world. With its distinctive thump, robust build quality, and classic styling, the Standard 350 offers an authentic riding experience that connects you to decades of motorcycling tradition.

Powered by a refined 349cc single-cylinder engine, the Standard 350 delivers a perfect balance of performance and efficiency. Whether you're navigating city streets or embarking on long highway journeys, this motorcycle provides the reliability and character that Royal Enfield is renowned for. Its simple yet elegant design philosophy ensures that every ride is a celebration of motorcycling heritage.
