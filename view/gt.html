About the Continental GT 650

The Royal Enfield Continental GT 650 is a modern interpretation of the classic café racer, blending timeless design with contemporary performance. Powered by Royal Enfield's acclaimed 650cc parallel-twin engine, the Continental GT delivers exhilarating performance while maintaining the brand's signature character. With its aggressive riding position, responsive handling, and distinctive styling, this motorcycle is built for riders who crave both style and substance.

The Continental GT 650 features premium components including USD front forks, Pirelli tires, and dual-channel ABS for confident braking. The café racer-inspired design includes clip-on handlebars, rear-set footpegs, and a single seat with an optional cowl. Whether carving through mountain roads or making a statement in the city, the Continental GT 650 offers an engaging riding experience that honors the café racer tradition while delivering modern reliability.
