About the Hunter 350

The Royal Enfield Hunter 350 represents a new generation of Royal Enfield motorcycles, designed specifically for urban riders who seek style, performance, and practicality. This roadster combines the brand's legendary heritage with contemporary design language, creating a motorcycle that's perfect for city commuting and weekend rides. With its lightweight construction and agile handling, the Hunter 350 offers an engaging riding experience.

Built on the proven J-platform, the Hunter 350 features modern amenities like an all-digital instrument cluster, Tripper navigation readiness, and USB charging port. The motorcycle's design philosophy focuses on simplicity and functionality, making it an ideal choice for young riders entering the Royal Enfield family. Its distinctive styling, refined engine, and competitive pricing make it a compelling option in the modern classic segment.
