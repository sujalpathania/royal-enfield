About the Super Meteor 650

The Royal Enfield Super Meteor 650 represents the pinnacle of cruiser motorcycling from the iconic British brand. Built around the proven 650cc parallel-twin engine, this motorcycle delivers exceptional highway performance with refined power delivery and smooth acceleration. The Super Meteor 650 is designed for riders who appreciate the classic cruiser aesthetic combined with modern engineering and reliability.

With its low-slung profile, forward controls, and relaxed riding position, the Super Meteor 650 is built for long-distance touring and highway cruising. The motorcycle features premium touches like LED lighting, Tripper navigation, and extensive customization options through Royal Enfield's 'Make-It-Yours' program. Whether you're cruising city boulevards or embarking on cross-country adventures, the Super Meteor 650 delivers comfort, style, and performance in equal measure.
