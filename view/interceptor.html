About the Interceptor 650

The Royal Enfield Interceptor 650 is a modern classic motorcycle that pays homage to the iconic Interceptors of the 1960s while delivering contemporary performance and reliability. Powered by Royal Enfield's 650cc parallel-twin engine, this motorcycle offers smooth power delivery and ample torque, making it perfect for both city commuting and highway cruising. With its timeless design and modern engineering, the Interceptor 650 brings back the golden era of motorcycling with 21st century refinement.

The Interceptor 650 features a comfortable upright riding position, premium suspension components, and responsive handling characteristics. The retro-inspired design includes chrome accents, spoke wheels (on some variants), and classic color schemes that recall Royal Enfield's rich heritage. Whether you're a seasoned rider or new to motorcycling, the Interceptor 650 offers an accessible yet engaging riding experience that celebrates the simple joy of riding.
